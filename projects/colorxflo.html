color x flo.

fall 2018

A response to the Emerald Necklace Conservancy's Fog x Flo project.

I wanted to focus on emotion and color; more specifically, how people relate certain colors with certain feelings.

My project, color x flo helps guide people through the process of associating a feeling they experienced at Fog x Flo with a color they saw.

process.

idea. I knew I wanted to create something that was interactive and stressed community.


functionality. I wanted users to be able to:

  • view other user's results in a gallery
  • upload their own photos
  • follow the step by step process to create a photo-word collage


    • steps to create a collage.

    1. pick a word/feeling they would use to describe Fog x Flo
    2. upload a photo from Fog x Flo or choose one of the defaults
    3. create a color palette by choosing different colors from the photo they chose
    4. change the color of the word they picked with the different colors from the palette they created
    5. submit their collage and/or see other people's collages in the gallery


    development. After completing the project, I decided to deploy to Heroku where it now lives.
